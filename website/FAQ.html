  • How is your lottery working ?

    ->> The lottery manager (the smart contract) create lottery pool. Each pool as its own parameters: max ticket and max time. When the pool is full or the maximum time is reach, the lottery manager randomly choose one ticket inside the pool, this ticket is the winner. This ticket is linked to the betting ETH address. This address automatically received fund from the contract.
    The john M pool is set without maximum amount it is run every 57600 block (around 10days).
    A new pool is created every time one is ended.

  • Do I need an account ?

    ->> NO accounts. NO deposits. Your sending address is your account.

  • Can I buy ticket for some one else ?
    ->> No you cannot ! As explain above in the warning section, Winning funds are always delivered at the address you bet with and only this address, their is no way to change this. This is to provide security and make sure no one can hack your funds.
